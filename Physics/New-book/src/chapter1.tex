\section*{Hydrogen Atom Problem}

The basis task is write down the Hamiltonian and then Schrodinger equation -
$$
H\psi = E\psi\qquad H = T+V
$$

$T=$ Kinetic energy

$V=$ Potential energy
$$
\therefore\quad H = -\dfrac{h^{2}\nabla^{2}}{2m}-\frac{1}{4\pi t_{0}}\dfrac{e^{2}}{r}
$$
